Here's a time line of Sheikh Mujib's life:
  • 1920 - Birth of Bangabandhu
  • 1947 - Protest for Bangla Language
  • 1952 - Language Movement
  • 1966 - 6 Demands Movement
  • 1969 - Movement of 1969
  • 1970 - General Election
  • 1971 - 7th March Speech & Liberation War
  • 1972 - Back in Independent Country
  • 1975 - Death of Bangabandhu
